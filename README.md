📊 This analysis aims to provide the management team with a complete **view of sales performance**.

**By analyzing the dashboard, they will be able to:**

- Track KPIs such as gross revenue, gross profit, gross margin, total orders, returned orders, and average order value.
- Monitor year-over-year and month-over-month growth for all KPIs.
- Visualize the market share of each product category, with underperforming categories highlighted when performance falls below expectations.
- Understand monthly gross revenue trends through a line chart.
- Clearly identify the top 5 products by gross profit.
- Analyze gross profit distribution by subcategory using a Pareto chart.

📈 **Key insights:**

- Products receiving discounts have become unprofitable and are negatively impacting overall profitability
- A small number of subcategories generate most of the profit (copiers, phones, bookcases, appliances and chairs)
- Despite $15M in revenue, only $1.47M becomes profit suggesting high costs or excessive discounting 
- Monthly revenue peaks in November and December likely due to holiday demand
- There is a noticeable dip around April and July which could indicate off‑season or weaker marketing campaigns
- The Tables subcategory shows a negative gross profit which is a clear red flag
- Phones, Chairs and Bookcases dominate market share

▶️ **How to Use This Project***

- Download the .pbix file from the dashboard folder
- Open it in Power BI Desktop
- Explore the pages to analyze KPIs, trends, and profitability insights
- Use filters to segment results by period, subcategory, product id or product name

🛠️**Tools used to build the project:**

- Power BI
- Excel for data processing
